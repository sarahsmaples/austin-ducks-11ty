Austin Holiday Lights Sing Along Tour

Celebrate the holidays with our Austin Lights Holiday Sing Along Tour!

Join us for a special nighttime holiday season sing along caroling tour! These tours departs from 113 E 5th Street (in front of the Austin Visitors Center) and take you through downtown Austin and finishes with a splash into Lake Austin. Each tour will be limited to 44 riders so be sure to book your reservation before space fills up!

Dates

  • December 21st at 5pm
  • December 22nd at 4pm & 6pm
  • December 23rd at 6pm
  • December 27th at 5pm

Pricing

  • Adults: $43.50
  • Kids: $28.50
