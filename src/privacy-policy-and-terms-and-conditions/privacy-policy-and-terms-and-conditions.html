Cancellation Policy

We offer full refunds if you cancel with more than 24 hours of notice from your tour time. There are NO REFUNDS if you cancel with less than 24 hours notice. To cancel reservations you must call 512-477-5274. We reserve the right to cancel tours for weather concerns or if less than 10 people are booked in which case a full refund or reschedule will be offered.

CAN SPAM Act

The CAN-SPAM Act is a law that sets the rules for commercial email, establishes requirements for commercial messages, gives recipients the right to have emails stopped from being sent to them, and spells out tough penalties for violations.

We collect your email address in order to:

  • Send you additional information related to your product and/or service.
